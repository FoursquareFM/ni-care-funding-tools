Temporary stays are treated differently

Your home is usually ignored for a temporary stay, and the Trust may use a different charging approach at first. The rules can change if a temporary stay becomes permanent.

Financial thresholds (2026/27)

Under £14,250: Trust usually covers your care. Between £14,250 and £23,250: you contribute on a sliding scale. Above £23,250: you fund privately unless Continuing Healthcare applies.

What this means for you

Savings above the upper threshold mean you fund care yourself at this stage. Consider how long the chosen fee level will remain affordable as savings reduce. If you choose a home that charges more than the Trust’s rate, you or your family may face top-up costs later.
